Must remain consistent throughout all materials:

- **The Skeleton** — Physical Infrastructure: ports, railways, energy lines, pipelines
- **The Nervous System** — ALIGN Intelligence: real-time sensing via AI agents and human networks
- **The Muscle** — Economic Execution: consortiums, SPVs, financial models

## Tone & Voice Rules

- **PE/Institutional Grade** — No startup language, no hype
- **Intelligence Agency Aesthetic** — Command center language, precision
- **Confidence without arrogance** — "We built this" not "We're the best"
- **Brevity + Impact** — Minimal text, maximum hierarchy
- **Active voice** — "ALIGN delivers" not "Certainty is delivered by ALIGN"
